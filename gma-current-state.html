Ivory Coast is regulated by the BCEAO (Banque Centrale des Etats de l'Afrique de l'Ouest) and subject to WAEMU monetary union rules. The following must be documented:

  • BCEAO Regulation R09/2010: External financial relations — capital controls for transfers outside WAEMU
  • STAR-UEMOA: RTGS system — settlement rules, cut-off times, value dating
  • SICA-UEMOA: ACH system — compensation windows, fund availability rules
  • Check clearing: BCEAO check clearing rules and timelines for Ivory Coast
  • AML/KYC: CENTIF (Cellule Nationale de Traitement des Informations Financieres) requirements
  • Mobile money: BCEAO e-money regulations (applicable to MTN Mobile Money)
  • OHADA Uniform Acts: Commercial law, company law — relevant for entity structure and IC transactions
